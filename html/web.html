About Me – Focused on Web Development

My name is Shah Faisal Aziz, a passionate and detail-oriented Computer Science Engineering student from East Champaran, Bihar. I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) at Rungta College of Engineering and Technology, Bhilai, where I have maintained a CGPA of 7.474.

I specialize in web development with a strong command over the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js). My journey has been fueled by structured learning and hands-on project development.

I have completed two comprehensive certification programs in Full Stack Web Development: a 4-month offline course from Coding Spoon and a 4-month online course from Simplilearn. These covered HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, AngularJS, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB.

I’ve built real-world projects like a Doctor Appointment Booking System and a Resume Analysis System where I developed frontend interfaces, backend APIs, and managed databases with a focus on responsive design and seamless user experience.

I am also an active competitive programmer with over 439 problems solved on platforms like LeetCode, Coding Ninjas, and GeeksforGeeks, which has strengthened my algorithmic and problem-solving skills.

With strong fundamentals, project experience, and a drive to keep learning, I aim to develop efficient, scalable, and impactful web applications.
